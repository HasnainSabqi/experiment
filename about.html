Compliance Standards & Mission

Gulf Medical Guide functions as a leading non-governmental informational registry dedicated to facilitating smooth health diagnostic tracking for travelers preparing to work or reside in the Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC) member states.

Our mission is to bridge the informational gap for expatriate workers navigating medical exams. We compile regional health rules, direct diagnostics centers, and verification requirements to ensure that applicants align perfectly with administrative protocols before embarking on their journey.

Through close observation of regulatory changes from health councils and embassies, we offer real-time updates and supportive digital features like our automated countdown helper, directing travelers securely to official portals for status search checks.

Important Notice & Disclaimer Gulf Medical Guide is an independent advisory and booking portal. All official search validation tasks are processed directly via secure redirects to official GCC Wafid/Health Council databases. We do not host patient biometric files on our server.
